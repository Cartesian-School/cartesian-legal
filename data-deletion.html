Data Deletion Instructions

At Cartesian School, we respect your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your data from our system, please follow the instructions below:

Steps to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to ssobo77@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. In the body of the email, please include:
    • Your full name
    • Your email address associated with your Cartesian School account
    • Your username (if applicable)
    • A clear statement that you are requesting the deletion of your data
  3. You will receive a confirmation email within 3 business days.
  4. We will process your request and delete your data within 30 days of receiving your request.

Please Note:

Deleting your data will result in the permanent loss of your account and all associated information. This action cannot be undone.

Some information may be retained for legal or administrative purposes, as required by law.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon your request, we will delete all personal information associated with your account, including but not limited to:

  • Your profile information
  • Your course progress and completion data
  • Your communication history with Cartesian School
  • Any user-generated content associated with your account

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this data deletion policy or the status of your request, please contact us at: ssobo77@gmail.com
